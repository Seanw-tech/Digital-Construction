Key Seismic Rules (NZS4219)
• Rigid ductwork with hanger >200mm from structure → must be restrained
• Pipework >50mm Ø + located >150mm from support → lateral bracing required
• Equipment in ceiling >10kg → independently fixed, 25mm clear of ceiling all round
Penetration Clearances at Structure / Partition Walls:
• Ductwork: 12mm  |  Pipework: 15mm  |  Cable Tray: 25mm
⚠ Ceiling hangers and braces are considered restrained for this table.

🔵 ELE Service Rules
  • 150mm min clearance between cable trays (125mm where constrained)
  • 150–300mm separation between ELV and LV cable trays
  • 150mm clearance above FCL for ceiling-mounted devices (PCA A-Grade)
  • 25mm penetration clearance at cable tray through structure
🟣 MEC Service Rules
  • ≤3m flexible duct length (target 2.5m)
  • 150mm min clearance between services and ceiling face
  • >200mm spacing between fire dampers
  • 12mm penetration clearance at ductwork through structure
  • Attenuators wider than parent duct — check spatial fit
🔴 HYD & FIR Service Rules
  • 50mm general clearance, all services to structure
  • 15mm penetration clearance at pipework through structure
  • >50mm Ø pipe → Major priority in Revizto  |  ≤50mm → Minor
  • Equal junctions → connect to main drain at ≥15° angle
  • 1200mm from fresh air grille → fire detector minimum offset (acoustic rule)
